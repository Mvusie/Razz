  1. 1 pound boneless,skinless(Raw)chicken breasts,cut into 2 1/2-inch chunks
  2. 2 stalks cerely, chopped
  3. 1/2 red bell pepper, sadded and chopped
  4. 4 to 6 green olives,pitted and minced
  5. 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  6. 1/2 to a whole apple,cored and chopped
  7. 1/3 head icedberg head lettuce,sliced and chopped
  8. 1L lemon juice

make the dressing

Prepare the chicken salad dressing in a large bowl.Mix together the mayonnaise, preserves,and a lemon juice.Taste for the proper balance of sweetness and acidity. the salad dressing should not be too sweet, nor too sour.

Add more preserves or lemon juice until you have reached the balance you want. Add salt and pepper to taste.
